## How to use this for fact-checking

When auditing our scraped numbers on the Other Social page:

1. **X family total** — check our `follower_snapshots` rows where `platform IN ('x', 'x_*')` for that channel; sum should be ≤ this column (we may be missing handles).
2. **IG / FB / TikTok / Threads** — single-account values; should match our scraped numbers within ±10% (counts shift daily).
3. **Δ and %** — the reporting cadence isn't documented but the magnitudes (most clubs +3-12%) suggest a 30-day window. Useful for sanity-checking growth claims in the AI note.

## Caveats

- Date of capture: not stated by source — values may be a few weeks stale by now.
- Column order is **inferred** from cross-checking against our own data; not labelled in the source paste. If a check fails systematically (e.g. our FB ≈ source's TikTok), the inferred order may be wrong.
- A few exotic ones (Cádiz CF total = 16.1M but only 0.3M+0.5M+4.7M+9.5M+1.1M = 16.1M — fine; their Real Sociedad's 15.6M TikTok looks oddly high vs the rest of our data — worth re-verifying when we sweep that club next).
